How to import worlds from other clients (1.5.2 only)
Step 1. Click "New World"
Step 2. Click "Load EPK File"
Step 3. Click "Continue"
How to export worlds to use in other clients
Step 1. Click "Backup"
Step 2. Click "Export to EPK", or if you want to play your world on non-web clients, click "Export to Vanilla".
